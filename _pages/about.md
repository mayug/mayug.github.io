For the past two years I have worked as a computer vision researcher at Qure.ai, Mumbai, where I have developed cranial bleed segmentation/ classification algorithms, using weak supervision and active learning techniques. Before this, I worked at the Computational Imaging Lab at IIT Madras under the guidance of Prof. Kaushik Mitra, where I worked on deep image priors for ill-posed inverse problems in computational imaging.

My research interests span the areas of machine learning and computer vision, specifically in making computer vision models learn with limited annotations.

Currently I am working on few-shot learning and how prior knowledge and reasoning can aid few shot performance.
